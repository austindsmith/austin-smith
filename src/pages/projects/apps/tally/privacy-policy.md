# Tally Privacy Policy

Tally does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Information Accessed

Tally uses Google Single Sign-On (SSO) to authenticate your account. It only accesses the Google Sheet(s) you choose to link for automating form filling.

## How Your Data Is Used

All data remains in your Google account. Tally does not send any information to its servers or third parties.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: brainslushee@proton.me.
